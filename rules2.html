Circuitrix

Circuitrix is a competition where electronics enthusiasts can demonstrate their skills and knowledge in circuits and electronics basics. It's a chance for participants to show off their understanding of the field and build a solid foundation in electronics.

Rules

Round 1:

  • 1. Each team can have a maximum of 2 participants.
  • 2. The first round consists of multiple-choice questions (MCQs).
  • 3. Teams will be scored based on the number of correct answers.
  • 4. The top 7 scoring teams will advance to the next round.
    •

Round 2:

  • 1. Participants in the second round will be given a circuit to work on.
  • 2. The objective is to complete the circuit and produce the correct output
  • 3. All the components required will be provided .
  • 4. The team with the shortest completion time will move to the next round.
    •

Round 3:

  • 1.In this round teams have to design circuit and show the output for the given problem statement.
  • 2.A total duration of 1 hours will be given to each team.
  • 3.Team should draw circuit diagram for the given problem statement.
  • 4.In case of tie-break between two or more teams, the one who completes the circuit and shows output with minimum time will be considered.
